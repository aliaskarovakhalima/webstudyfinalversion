Welcome to the WebStudy portal!

The place where everyone can find information about web-sites. We are a team of KazNPU students who developed this website as part of our thesis. The purpose of the development of this site was to contribute to solving problems related to the problems of teaching computer science and in particular the sections of web design.

Who is this site for?

For students

Students of the socio-humanitarian field of study will be able to find additional information on how to create websites using special constructors. The natural-mathematical field of study will be able to find additional information on how to create websites on HTML and CSS.

For teachers

Teachers can find lesson plans of the section, additional materials and useful digital resources
